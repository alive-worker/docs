About & Disclaimer

AI Access Guide is an English-language resource focused on subscribing to and paying for mainstream AI services when your local card, bank, or region gets in the way — our goal is to help individuals and teams subscribe smoothly, reliably, and within the rules.

1. What This Site Is

We cover subscription sign-up, regional availability, payment channels, and team cost management for services like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Midjourney. Every article starts from "how to complete a real, working subscription smoothly and reliably" — we don't encourage or provide instructions for evading any platform's risk controls or the laws of your jurisdiction.

2. Content & Editorial Team

Articles are written and maintained by the "AI Access Guide editorial team" for informational purposes only — nothing here is investment, financial, tax, or legal advice. The team tracks subscription pricing changes, regional policy shifts, and payment-channel availability across platforms on an ongoing basis.

Our content follows these principles:

  • Primary sources first: for pricing, plan tiers, and regional restrictions, the official pages of each AI platform, payment provider, and tool are authoritative — our articles only organize and explain that information.
  • Verified steps: walkthroughs for virtual card setup, stablecoin bridging, and similar operational content are checked step by step against real workflows, not written in the abstract.
  • Pre-publish review: before publishing or updating, we re-check pricing, fees, and supported chains/card networks against official sources.

Pricing, plan tiers, regional availability, and payment requirements change — always confirm with the official page before you act. If you spot an error or something out of date, please reach out using the contact details below.

3. Editorial & Update Policy

Every article is timestamped with its publish and last-updated dates. For information that can affect your decisions — subscription pricing, payment policy, regional restrictions, stablecoin transfers, virtual card usage — we re-verify where possible whenever we update, and keep risk notes in the body text. If you notice outdated, inaccurate, or changed third-party information, please email us; we prioritize anything affecting fund safety, account security, or compliance judgment.
